Smart irrigation controllers use advanced technology to optimize water usage:

  • Weather Integration: Automatically adjusts schedules based on local weather forecasts
  • Soil Sensors: Measures actual soil moisture to prevent overwatering
  • Seasonal Programming: Reduces watering during cooler months
  • Rain Skip: Postpones watering when rain is detected
  • Remote Monitoring: Control and monitor your system from anywhere
Typical Water Savings: 30-50% reduction in water usage while maintaining healthier landscapes and reducing monthly water bills.
