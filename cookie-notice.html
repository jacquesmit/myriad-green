Cookie Notice & POPIA Compliance | Myriad Green

Effective Date: 25 July 2025

Why We Use Cookies

At Myriad Green, we use cookies to enhance your experience across our irrigation, landscaping, water filtration, and rainwater harvesting services. Cookies help us deliver fast, secure, and personalized solutions for your garden, home, or business.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Enable core site features such as navigation, booking modals, and secure checkout for irrigation and landscaping products.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Track usage patterns, service area interest, and product popularity (e.g., Google Analytics) to improve our irrigation, plumbing, and water tank offerings.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as dark/light mode, selected service area, and saved cart items.
  • Advertising & Social Media Cookies: Help us show relevant offers for irrigation systems, water tanks, and landscaping packages on platforms like Facebook and Google.

Third-Party Cookies

We partner with trusted providers (Google, Stripe, Facebook) for analytics, payments, and social media integration. These services may set their own cookies when you interact with booking, checkout, or contact forms.

POPIA & Your Privacy Rights

Myriad Green complies with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We respect your privacy and only collect data necessary to deliver our irrigation, landscaping, and water solutions. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Managing Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect booking, checkout, and personalized service features. For help, see our FAQ or contact us.

Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies for service delivery, analytics, and personalization, unless you disable cookies in your browser.
