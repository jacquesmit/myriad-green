Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover for irrigation services?

We provide irrigation services across all major areas of Gauteng, including Johannesburg (Sandton, Rosebank, Melrose, Houghton), Pretoria (Waterkloof, Faerie Glen, Brooklyn, Menlyn), and surrounding suburbs. Our technicians are strategically located for rapid response throughout the province.

How do smart irrigation controllers save water?

Smart controllers use real-time weather data, soil moisture sensors, and seasonal programming to automatically adjust watering schedules. They skip watering when rain is forecast, reduce runtime during cooler months, and can be monitored remotely. Most clients see 30-50% water savings within the first year.

Do you service irrigation systems installed by other companies?

Yes, we service and repair irrigation systems regardless of who installed them originally. Our technicians are experienced with all major brands including Rain Bird, Hunter, Orbit, and Irritrol. We provide diagnostic assessments and can upgrade older systems with modern components.
