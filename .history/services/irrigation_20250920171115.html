How Our Irrigation Process Works

  1. Site assessment: We evaluate your property's water pressure, soil conditions, plant requirements, and existing infrastructure to design the optimal system layout.
  2. Custom quote & plan: Detailed proposal with zone mapping, component specifications, timeline, and transparent pricing including any municipal compliance requirements.
  3. Professional installation: Certified technicians install your system with minimal landscape disruption, testing each zone and programming controllers to your specific needs.
  4. Optimization & handover: System fine-tuning, user training on controller operation, and setup of ongoing maintenance schedule to ensure long-term performance.
