5 Smart Irrigation Tips for a Drought-Resistant Garden

Published July 2025 by Myriad Green

South Africa faces unique water challenges. Here are five expert tips to help your garden thrive year-round without wasting a drop:

  • Use Drip Irrigation: Delivers water directly to plant roots, minimizing evaporation.
  • Install Timers: Water early morning or late evening to avoid midday loss.
  • Group Plants by Need: This prevents overwatering drought-tolerant species.
  • Use Mulch: Retains soil moisture and prevents weed growth.
  • Regular Maintenance: Check for leaks, clogs, and pressure drops often.
